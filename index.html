  • Energy Efficient Fan

    The Energy Efficient Fan represents a significant advancement in household ventilation technology. It serves not only as a cooling device but also as a multi-functional appliance. The fan is designed to intelligently adjust its speed based on the number of occupants in a room, ensuring optimal comfort and energy efficiency.

    Furthermore, it features an automatic shutdown mechanism when the room is vacant, reducing unnecessary power consumption. Additionally, the fan is equipped with a mosquito arrester, enhancing its utility by providing insect protection. This innovative fan not only keeps spaces comfortable but also promotes energy conservation and improves the overall living experience by addressing various needs simultaneously.

  • Bike Solar plantation

    The Solar Energy Charging Plantation is a unique design consisting of branches, a trunk, and a base unit, resembling a tree. Each branch on the trunk is equipped with two grid solar panels, similar to leaves, and they are adjustable to capture sunlight from various angles.

    These solar panels harness solar energy and convert it into electrical power. This innovative approach mimics the efficiency of natural photosynthesis, allowing the tree-like structure to efficiently generate electricity by responding to sunlight throughout the day. This sustainable and aesthetically pleasing system provides a green energy source while harmonizing with the environment.

  • Confined Air turbine generator

    The Confined Air Turbine Generator is an ingenious system designed for efficient power generation from airflow. It operates by first directing incoming air through a convergent nozzle, creating a controlled and pressurized flow. This focused air stream is then harnessed to drive an air turbine with exceptional precision.

    This turbine, in turn, powers a micro generator, efficiently converting the kinetic energy from the airflow into electricity. The compact and streamlined design of this assembly makes it an environmentally friendly and space-saving solution for electricity generation, with applications ranging from renewable energy sources to ventilation systems and beyond, promoting sustainability and energy efficiency.

  • Energy-less Battery Fire Extinguisher

    The Energy-less Battery Fire Extinguisher is an innovative safety device designed to enhance battery safety and prevent fire accidents, particularly in automobiles. It employs carbon dioxide (CO2) as a highly effective fire suppressant agent, known for its rapid fire extinguishing properties.

    This extinguisher plays a crucial role in extending the lifespan of batteries by efficiently dissipating the heat generated during their operation, reducing the risk of thermal runaway incidents. By mitigating the potential fire hazards associated with batteries, it not only safeguards human lives but also protects valuable assets and prevents costly accidents, making it a vital component of battery-powered systems and vehicles.
